#+title:      RLTA Statute in Derogation of the Common Law and Strictly Construed
#+date:       [2023-12-05 Tue 00:45]
#+filetags:   :rlta:constructiion:strict:
#+identifier: 20231205T004553

Because "[c]hapters 59.12 and 59.18 RCW are statutes in derogation of
the common law," they "are strictly construed in favor of the tenant."
_Randy Reynolds & Assocs., Inc. v. Harmon_, 193 Wn.2d 143, 156 (2019).
