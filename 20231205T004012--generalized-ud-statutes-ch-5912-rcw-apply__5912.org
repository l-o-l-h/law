#+title:      Generalized UD Statutes Ch 59.12 RCW Apply
#+date:       [2023-12-05 Tue 00:40]
#+filetags:   :5912:
#+identifier: 20231205T004012

"The procedures set forth in the generalized unlawful detainer
statutes, Chapter 59.12 RCW, 'apply to the extent that they are not
supplanted by those found in the Residential Landlord-Tenant Act
(RLTA).'"  _Randy Reynolds & Assocs., Inc. v. Harmon_, 193 Wn.2d 143,
156 (2019) (quoting _Housing Auth. of City of Pasco & Franklin County
v. Pleasant_, 126 Wn. App. 382, 390 (2005)).
