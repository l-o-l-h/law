* RCW 59.18.430. Applicability to prior, existing or future leases

RCW 59.18.010 through  59.18.360 and 59.18.900 shall not  apply to any
lease entered  into prior  to July  16, 1973.  All provisions  of this
chapter shall apply  to any lease or periodic tenancy  entered into on
or subsequent to July 16, 1973.

The argument is that this statute forbids the use of the ejectment
action in residential leases governed by the RLTA.
