* Upsolve

Hi  Lincoln!  right now  because  our  bankruptcy program  is  closed,
unfortunately I don't have any attorneys doing pro bono cases. the one
resource we give folks is Upsolve  - it's a nonprofit where low income
folks can file for bankruptcy for free/at a reduced cost. their online
tool will  help determine whether  filing for bankruptcy is  the right
choice and,  if so, will  help you along  the process. we've  had good
feedback that it was quite helpful!
