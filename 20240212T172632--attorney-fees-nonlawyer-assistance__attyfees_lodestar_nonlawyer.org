Costs, Fees, and Sanctions
102k780Paralegals, assistants, and secretaries

Fees of nonlawyer personnel may be properly requested as part of
attorney fee award.


- McGreevy v. Oregon Mut. Ins. Co., 90 Wn. App. 283, 951 P.2d 798
  (1998), disapproved of by Panorama Vill. Condo. Owners Ass'n Bd. of
  Directors v. Allstate Ins. Co., 144 Wn.2d 130, 26 P.3d 910 (2001)



Costs, Fees, and Sanctions
102k780Paralegals, assistants, and secretaries

Award of fees for services performed by nonlawyer could not be made
based solely on documentation of number of hours worked and hourly
rate without consideration of paralegal's qualifications and nature of
work performed.


- McGreevy v. Oregon Mut. Ins. Co., 90 Wn. App. 283, 951 P.2d 798
  (1998), disapproved of by Panorama Vill. Condo. Owners Ass'n Bd. of
  Directors v. Allstate Ins. Co., 144 Wn.2d 130, 26 P.3d 910 (2001)
