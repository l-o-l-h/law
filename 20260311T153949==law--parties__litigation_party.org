* Parties | Key Number Topics 287 k 51(4) | Persons Who May Be Brought In, and Grounds in General

Under Rem. & Bal.Code, §§ 191,  196, 265, 273, RCW 4.08.080, 4.08.130,
4.32.100, 4.32.090, a defendant in an action by an assignee of a chose
in action may require the court to bring in the assignor.
- _State v. Superior Court for King Cnty._, 67 Wash. 355, 121 P. 847 (1912)
